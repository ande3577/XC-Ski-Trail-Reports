Cross-country Ski Trail Conditions
December 12, 2012

Park hours are 5:00 AM–10:00 PM. Facility hours vary; see individual listings. For additional information, call the Trail Hotline at 763.559.6778.

Ski trail maps are available on each park page.

Baker Park Reserve

Prairie, Island, Hill, Timber and Fairway trails are packed, tracked and in GOOD condition for both skating and traditional skiing. The Evergreen trail and Lesson area are CLOSED. The Chalet is open from 9:00 am.m to 5:00 p.m. daily. Rentals are available. Updated 12/11/12

Carver Park Reserve

All trails are packed, tracked and in FAIR condition for both skating and traditional skiing. The sliding hill is OPEN. Equipment rentals will be available 9:00 a.m. - 5:00 p.m. weekends only, beginning Saturday, December 15. Updated 12/12/12

Cleary Lake Regional Park

Cross-country ski trails are PACKED and in FAIR condition for skating. Poplar and Lakeside trails are TRACKED and in POOR condition for traditional. Equipment rentals are available. The visitor center is OPEN 9:00 a.m. - 7:00 p.m., daily. Trail lights are ON until 10:00 p.m. Updated 12/11/12

Eagle Lake Regional Park

Eagle trail is packed, tracked and in GOOD condition for both skating and traditional skiing. Pike trail and the practice loop will by finished and in GOOD condition Wednesday. Updated 12/11/12

Elm Creek Park Reserve

1.3 mile of the Valley Trail is packed, tracked and in GOOD condition for both skating and traditional skiing. The Northern Lights Trail is packed, tracked and in GOOD condition for both skating and traditional skiing. No other trails have been groomed. Rentals are available. The Chalet is open 9:00 a.m. to 9:00 p.m. Lights are on until 10:00 p.m. Sliding hill is open. Updated 12/11/12

French Regional Park

The Lagoon, Meadow, Skyling, Lakeview, Medicine Loop and Lesson Area area all packed, tracked and in GOOD condition for both skating and traditional. Challenge Hill is CLOSED. Sliding hill is groomed and OPEN. Updated 12/11/12

Glen Lake Golf Center

Cross Country ski trail is CLOSED.

Hyland Lake Park Reserve

All Trails are PACKED; TRACKED and in GOOD condition for skating, FAIR for traditional skiing. Rentals are available. The sliding hill is OPEN. Visitor Center closes at 9:00 p.m. Trail lights are ON. Updated 12/12/12

Murphy-Hanrehan Park Reserve

Cross-country ski trails are CLOSED.
